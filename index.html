About FLOP Map

A public, interactive 3D map of every known AI compute cluster on earth — all 691 sites tracked by Epoch AI's GPU Clusters dataset, covering ≈55 GW of power and ≈79M H100-equivalent chips. Built to make the infrastructure behind the AI boom visible.
How it works
Each dot is a cluster (points view), or rolled up by geography (heatmap / hex). Click a dot for full specs: power, chips, FLOP/s, cost, sources. Filter by status, operator, country, sector, or year. Pin up to 4 clusters to compare side-by-side. Press ? for keyboard shortcuts.
Data & caveats
Data comes from Epoch AI under CC BY. 253 anonymised Chinese clusters share one centroid (35.5N, 101.9E) — heatmap reads this correctly but the raw dot count is misleading. Many planned sites lack power or FLOP/s estimates. Not every cluster on earth is here; this is the best public dataset we have.
